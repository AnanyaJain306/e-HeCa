WHO WE ARE?

The e-HeCa team consists of first year B-Tech CSE students of Bennett University.We started e-HeCa as our first semester project.We were motivated and inspired by our professors to make a socially beneficial project that is viable to help the people in any which way possible. Hence the team came up with the topic to work on healthcare. After a lot of research that went into this project we were able to come up with a medicine reminder web application that had some other basic features.

VISION BEHIND e-HeCa!

The main objective of this project was to help old people or senior citizens who at that stage of their lives become forgetful and are unable to go and buy their own medicine or even visit the doctor.
